How to use

Download a copy of your instagram data. Instagram will send it to your email.

Unzip the archive you downloaded.

Click choose file on the left side of the screen and select the file you wish to use. Your follower data is stored in a folder where you extracted the downloaded .zip archive. You should look for connections.json.

Click the button you wish to execute.

Get Followers - Displays a list of people who are your followers.

Get Following - Displays a list of people that you follow.

Get non-followers - Displays a list of people that you follow, but they don't follow you.

Get sent requests - Displays a list of people that have pending follow request from you.

Get Close Friends - Displays a list of your close friends

Get non-close followers - Displays a list of people who are your followers, but who are not in your close friends.

Get close non-followers - Displays a list of accounts who are in your close friends list, but who don't follow you.

Clear List - Erases the list from the screen.
